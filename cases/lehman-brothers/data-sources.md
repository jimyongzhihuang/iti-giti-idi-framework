# Lehman Brothers Data Sources

This file records the public data sources to be used for the Lehman Brothers ITI–gITI–IDI case study.

The purpose is to separate raw public evidence from interpretation, index construction, and final analysis.

## Source Categories

### 1. Financial Statements and Annual Reports

Potential materials include Lehman Brothers annual reports, quarterly filings, balance sheets, income statements, cash flow statements, leverage disclosures, asset classification notes, and liquidity disclosures.

These materials will support the ITI layer by showing the formal financial structure and expected institutional condition before failure.

### 2. Regulatory and Public Inquiry Materials

Potential materials include bankruptcy examiner reports, congressional hearing materials, SEC-related materials, Federal Reserve materials, and public investigation records.

These materials will support the institutional expectation layer and help identify what regulators, auditors, and market institutions expected or failed to capture.

### 3. Market and Outcome Data

Potential materials include stock price movements, credit spreads, CDS data, liquidity indicators, market capitalization changes, counterparty exposure signals, and bankruptcy timeline data.

These materials will support the gITI layer by showing realized outcome-side tension.

### 4. Timeline Materials

Potential materials include key dates before and after the bankruptcy filing, liquidity events, rating changes, market reaction dates, rescue negotiation dates, and bankruptcy filing records.

These materials will support the alignment between expected strain and realized distortion.

## Data Handling Rule

Only public and verifiable sources will be used.

Each data point should be recorded with:

- source title;
- publication date or filing date;
- institution or author;
- data type;
- relevance to ITI, gITI, or IDI;
- link or citation location;
- extraction note.

## Current Status

Data collection has not yet been completed. This file will be updated as sources are identified, verified, and mapped into the framework.
