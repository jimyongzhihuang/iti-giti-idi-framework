# Lehman Brothers Case Note

This folder will document the Lehman Brothers case as the first applied financial-system case for the ITI–gITI–IDI framework.

The purpose of the case is not to retell the financial crisis in general terms. The purpose is to test whether institutional distortion can be made visible by comparing expected institutional strain with realized outcome-side tension.

## Case Function

The Lehman Brothers case will be used to examine:

- expected institutional strain before failure;
- realized market, balance-sheet, liquidity, and regulatory outcomes;
- the gap between formal institutional expectation and actual systemic result;
- the possibility of calculating an Institutional Distortion Index.

## Working Question

Can the ITI–gITI–IDI framework identify institutional distortion before or during a major financial failure?
